Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses - Naming/Starter Deck Simulator


This is a Work-In-Progress. Results may not be accurate as of yet.

This simulator is to help figure out which three starter decks you can choose from out of the 17 that are in the game. This is determined by what name you decide to call yourself (check here for more info "Starter decks and names"). Once a result is found, click on each starter deck to see the list of cards and fusions that it contains.
