Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses - Naming/Starter Deck Simulator


In the game, the player's name determines what 3 starter decks they can choose from out of the 17 that are in the game (check here for more info under "Starter decks and names"). This simulator can help figure out what starter decks the player can get.

A maximum of 12 characters can be used. Click on the starter deck for more info in the Deck Builder.

If you want a specific starter deck while generally keeping the same name, add 1 letter from A-P. Keep cycling through the extra letter until you've reached the desired starter deck.
