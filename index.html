Yu-Gi-Oh! The Duelists of the Roses - Fusion Simulator

For all intents and purposes, cards that include "Elegant Egotist", "Cocoon of Evolution" and "Metalmorph" as their "SPECIAL POWER UP" will count as fusions. "Insect Imitation" will not be included since it fuses into a random card.

The light-blue box labeled "Card on Field" is for a card that is already in your Summoning Area, and it will always be placed as the first card in the fusion order. The simulator will still display options for whether or not you choose to use the card on the field. All other boxes can have cards be arranged in any order, and they will be calculated for any possible combinations from the hand. Blank inputs will be ignored, but a minimum of 2 cards is needed to perform a fusion.

Enter the names of the card or their ID number. Use this guide for a general fusion list.

A general rule of thumb is that a fusion material's original ATK cannot be higher than the fusion monster's ATK (with a few, rare exceptions).

Some fusions will have minimum ATK or LV requirement (i.e. 1500+ ATK or LV 5+). Only the fusion materials' original type, ATK, attribute, etc. matters. Changing a monster's stat will not affect the fusion result.

Some fusions requires a monster from the Female, Elf, or Turtle archetypes. Elf monsters have "Elf" in its name ("Celtic Guardian" is considered as an elf for fusions), Turtle monsters have "Turtle" or "Tortoise" in its name, and Female monsters can be identified if they can equip female Power Ups like "Cyber Shield". Fusions involving these archetypes as fusion materials will typically be placed in a higher priority than fusions that exclusively deal with a monster's type. For example, a female, Fairy with less than 1300 ATK and a Fish will prioritize fusing for "Amazon of the Seas" (Female + Fish) over "7 Colored Fish" (Fairy + Fish).

Due to how the game is programmed, there are some inconsistencies of some card combinations that should and shouldn't work. Please use the fusion simulator to double check.
